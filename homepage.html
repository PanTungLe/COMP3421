About Me

Hi! My name is Pan Tung-Le, or some people call me Tung-Le Pan in certain parts of the world. The respective Chinese characters corrensponding to my name is 潘同樂 (IPA: [pʰän⁵⁵ tʰʊŋ³⁵ lɤ⁵¹]) and I am a penultimate year student at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University studying Chinese and Bilingual Studies while doing a minor in Computing.

I hail from the subtropical island of Taiwan where I spent the first 18 years of my life, in the port city of Keelung. After graduating from high school, I decided to pursue my studied in Hong Kong due to its cosmpolitan atmosphere and its fast-paced lifestyle which I consider to be suitable to my personality.

I enjoy travelling since it is an experience through which one can come across diversity face-to-face, and the sensory experiences and revelations one encounters these trips are incomparable to those that are acquired through books. The following are the places that I've travelled to:
